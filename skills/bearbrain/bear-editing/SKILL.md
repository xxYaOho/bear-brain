---
name: bearbrain/bear-editing
description: Bear note editing protocol for Bear-Brain notes. Use when creating, updating, appending, replacing, or restructuring Bear notes while preserving agreed BB formatting, anchors, Related Notes list style, and snapshot-plus-overwrite behavior for long-lived notes.
---

Use Bear title fields for note titles and Bear tags for tags.

Follow these defaults:
- overwrite long-lived planning or governance notes after snapshotting
- append to daily logs and running notes
- update `Related Notes` as nested bullet lists, not tables
- mark inactive entry points with strikethrough instead of deleting them
